### Core Concept
As an employee of AllRisk Solution, your job is to process reimbursement claims while following your company's increasingly inhumane regulations... Report your work to your overly positive manager, drink coffee with your colleagues, and most importantly investigate the otherworldly happening in the office.  

### Target Audience
- Players who enjoy atmospheric horror, and the narrative puzzle simulator games, like Paper's Please and Stanley Parable.
- Variety streamers.

### Platform & Technology
- **Platform**: PC (Steam), with potential console ports
- **Engine**: Unity 6
- **Target Performance**: 60+ FPS on low-end hardware
